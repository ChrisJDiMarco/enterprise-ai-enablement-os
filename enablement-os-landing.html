The problem

Most companies don't fail at AI because they lack models. They fail because use cases are scattered, pilots are unmeasured, tools are ungoverned, and value is impossible to prove.

The model is not the system. The system is the harness around the model — identity, context, policy, tools, approvals, evals, audit, and ROI. That is what this platform is.
